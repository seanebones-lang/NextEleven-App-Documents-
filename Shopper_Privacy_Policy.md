## Introduction

Shopper ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide

- **Shopping Cart Data**: Items, prices, discounts, coupons, and tax information you enter or scan
- **Location Data**: State selection and ZIP code for tax calculation purposes (stored locally only)
- **Camera Data**: Images captured for barcode and QR code scanning (processed on-device, not stored)

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, iOS version (for compatibility purposes)
- **Usage Data**: App functionality usage patterns (for app improvement)
- **Crash Reports**: Error logs and crash information (if you enable crash reporting)

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ Payment information
- ❌ Location tracking or GPS coordinates
- ❌ Contacts or address book information
- ❌ Photos or images from your photo library
- ❌ Biometric data
- ❌ Advertising identifiers
- ❌ Third-party tracking data

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Storage (On Your Device)

- **Shopping History**: Your shopping cart data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData
- **Preferences**: Your state selection and tax preferences are saved locally
- **Session Data**: Current shopping session data is maintained in memory

### AI Features

- **xAI Grok API**: When you use AI-powered features (shopping suggestions, coupon interpretation), your shopping cart data is sent to xAI's Grok API for processing
- **API Key**: The app uses either your provided xAI API key or an internal API key for AI features
- **Data Transmission**: Only shopping cart data (items, prices, discounts) is sent to xAI; no personal information is included
- **No Storage**: xAI processes your data but does not store it according to their privacy policy

### Third-Party Services

- **xAI Grok API**: Used for AI-powered shopping assistance
- **Product Lookup Services**: Used for barcode scanning to identify products (optional feature)
- **Tax Lookup Services**: Used for accurate tax rate calculations (optional feature)

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- All shopping cart data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
- Data is encrypted at rest using iOS device encryption
- Data is not synced to iCloud unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync (future feature)

### Data Retention

- Shopping history is retained on your device until you delete it
- You can clear all data at any time through the app settings
- Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data

### Security Measures

- All data processing occurs on your device or through secure HTTPS connections
- We do not transmit personal information to third parties
- Camera access is used only for scanning barcodes and QR codes; images are not stored

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control

- **View Your Data**: You can view all stored shopping history within the app
- **Delete Your Data**: You can delete individual shopping sessions or clear all data
- **Export Your Data**: You can export shopping receipts as PDF files

### California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Know if your personal information is sold or disclosed
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information
- Access your personal information
- Request deletion of your personal information

**Note**: Shopper does not sell personal information. We only collect shopping cart data which is stored locally on your device.

### European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Erase your data
- Restrict processing of your data
- Data portability
- Object to processing

**Note**: Shopper processes data locally on your device. AI features may transmit shopping cart data to xAI's servers, but no personal information is included.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Shopper is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at info@mothership-ai.com.

## Camera Permissions

Shopper requests camera access to:
- Scan product barcodes for quick price entry
- Scan QR codes and barcodes for coupon codes

**Important:**
- Camera access is optional; you can use the app without it
- Images are processed on-device and are not stored
- Camera data is not transmitted to our servers or third parties
- You can revoke camera access at any time through iOS Settings
