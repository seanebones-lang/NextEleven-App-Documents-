# ThriftFlip Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2, 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 2, 2025  
**Version:** 2.0

## Introduction

NextEleven Software Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") operates the ThriftFlip mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Email address (optional)
- Display name (optional)
- Profile preferences

**Product Data:**
- Product scans (barcodes, photos)
- Saved products and flip records
- Purchase costs and sale prices
- Notes and descriptions
- Watchlist items

**User Preferences:**
- Default profit margins
- Preferred selling platforms
- Notification settings
- Vendor search preferences

### Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Device identifiers (for crash reporting only)

**Usage Data:**
- Features used
- Scan frequency
- Performance metrics (anonymized)
- Crash reports (anonymized)

**Camera Access:**
- Required for barcode scanning and product photography
- Images processed locally on device
- Images uploaded to Grok AI only with your explicit action
- Images not stored on our servers

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Scan and identify products
   - Search marketplace prices
   - Calculate profit potential
   - Store your flip records

2. **AI-Powered Features**
   - Product identification via Grok 4.1
   - Condition assessment
   - Price predictions
   - Market analysis

3. **Improve User Experience**
   - Remember your preferences
   - Optimize scan performance
   - Fix bugs and crashes
   - Enhance features

4. **Communication**
   - Send price drop alerts (if enabled)
   - Notify about app updates
   - Respond to support requests

## Third-Party Services

### Grok AI (xAI)

**Purpose:** AI-powered product analysis and market intelligence  
**Data Shared:** Product images, barcode data (only when you scan)  
**Your Control:** You provide your own API key  
**Privacy Policy:** [x.ai/privacy](https://x.ai/privacy)

**Important:** 
- You pay xAI directly
- Your API key is stored in iOS Keychain (never on our servers)
- Images sent to Grok are processed per xAI's privacy policy
- You can remove your API key anytime

### eBay Finding API

**Purpose:** Marketplace price discovery  
**Data Shared:** Product barcodes/keywords  
**Privacy:** No personal information shared  
**Policy:** [ebay.com/privacy](https://ebay.com/privacy)

### Other APIs (Optional)

If you configure additional vendor APIs, those services may collect data according to their policies.

**If iCloud sync is added in future:**

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control

**You Can:**
- ✅ View all your stored data in the app
- ✅ Edit or delete any flip records
- ✅ Export your data (CSV/PDF)
- ✅ Delete your entire database
- ✅ Remove your Grok API key
- ✅ Disable camera access (Settings > Privacy)

### Data Deletion

**To Delete All Data:**
1. Open ThriftFlip
2. Go to Settings
3. Tap "Reset All Settings"
4. Delete the app to remove all local data

**What Happens:**
- All flip records deleted
- All scanned products removed
- All preferences reset
- API keys removed from Keychain

## Security

### How We Protect Your Data

**Encryption:**
- API keys stored in iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted)
- Network requests use HTTPS/TLS
- Local database encrypted by iOS

**Access Control:**
- No backend servers (your data never leaves your device)
- Face ID/Touch ID protection (iOS system-level)
- App sandbox isolation

**Best Practices:**
- Regular security audits
- Swift 6 strict concurrency (prevents race conditions)
- Input validation on all data
- Secure coding standards (OWASP)

### Your Responsibility

- Keep your device secure (passcode/biometrics)
- Don't share your Grok API key
- Keep iOS updated for security patches
- Use strong device encryption

## Camera and Photo Access

**What We Access:**
- Camera: For scanning barcodes and taking product photos
- Photo Library: For selecting existing product images (optional)

**How We Use It:**
- Process images locally for barcode detection
- Send images to Grok AI only when you scan (with your API key)
- Images not uploaded to our servers
- Images not stored unless you save the product

**Your Control:**
- Revoke camera access: iOS Settings > ThriftFlip > Camera
- Revoke photo access: iOS Settings > ThriftFlip > Photos
- App will prompt for permission when needed
