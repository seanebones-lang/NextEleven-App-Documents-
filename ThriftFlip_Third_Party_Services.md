## Why Two Separate Services?

### Transparency and Control

**Benefits to You:**

**1. Pay Only for What You Use**
- Light user: ~$0.02/month for Grok
- Heavy user: ~$0.10/month for Grok
- No markup from us

**2. No Hidden Costs**
- Clear separation
- Two bills, two companies
- You control both

**3. Flexibility**
- Use barcode scanner without Grok (free)
- Add Grok only if you want AI features
- Remove Grok anytime

**4. Cost Efficiency**
- Traditional apps: $20-50/month all-inclusive (you pay even if you don't use it)
- ThriftFlip: $9.99/month + ~$0.03/month Grok = ~$10.02/month (pay for actual usage)
