## Welcome to Shopper!

This guide will help you get started with Shopper and troubleshoot common issues you may encounter.

## Getting Started

### First Launch

1. **Open the App:** Tap the Shopper icon on your home screen
2. **Grant Permissions:** If prompted, allow camera access for barcode/QR scanning (optional)
3. **Start Shopping:** You're ready to use the app!

### Basic Usage

#### Adding Items

1. **Enter Price:** Type the item price in the "Add Item" section
2. **Add Discounts (Optional):**
   - Enter a percentage discount (e.g., "20" for 20% off)
   - Or enter a fixed discount amount (e.g., "5.00" for $5 off)
3. **Add Item:** Tap "Add Item" to add it to your cart

#### Using the Scanner

**For Products:**
1. Tap the barcode icon next to the price field
2. Point your camera at a product barcode
3. The app will automatically look up the product and enter the price

**For Coupons:**
1. Tap the QR code icon in the "Coupon Code" section
2. Point your camera at a coupon QR code or barcode
3. The app will automatically scan and apply the coupon

#### Setting Tax

1. **By State:** Select your state from the dropdown menu
2. **By ZIP Code:** Enter your ZIP code for more accurate tax rates
3. Tax is automatically calculated and applied to your total

#### Applying Coupons

1. **Manual Entry:** Type the coupon code in the "Coupon Code" field
2. **Scan:** Tap the QR code icon to scan a coupon
3. **Apply:** Tap "Apply" to apply the coupon to your cart
4. The app will automatically parse and apply the discount

#### AI Features

1. **Shopping Suggestions:** Tap "Ask Grok for Suggestions" to get AI-powered shopping advice
2. **Coupon Interpretation:** The app automatically uses AI to interpret complex coupon codes
3. **Tips:** AI can suggest better ways to apply discounts and save money

#### Viewing History

1. Tap "History" in the navigation bar
2. View your past shopping sessions
3. Tap a session to view details or resume shopping

#### Sharing Receipts

1. Tap "Share" in the summary section
2. Choose how to share (Messages, Email, AirDrop, etc.)
3. A PDF receipt will be generated and shared

## Common Tasks

### Clearing Your Cart

- Tap "Clear Cart" to remove all items
- Individual items can be removed by swiping left

### Saving Your Session

- Your cart is automatically saved every 30 seconds
- You can resume your session from History

### Exporting Data

- Use the Share button to export receipts as PDF
- Shopping history is stored locally on your device

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Issues

**Problem:** Camera preview is black or not showing

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Permissions:**
   - Go to iOS Settings → Shopper → Camera
   - Ensure camera access is enabled
2. **Restart the App:**
   - Close the app completely
   - Reopen and try again
3. **Restart Your Device:**
   - Sometimes a device restart resolves camera issues
4. **Check iOS Version:**
   - Ensure you're running iOS 17.0 or later

**Problem:** Barcode/QR code not scanning

**Solutions:**
1. **Lighting:** Ensure adequate lighting
2. **Distance:** Hold device 6-12 inches from barcode
3. **Focus:** Wait for the camera to focus
4. **Clean Lens:** Clean your camera lens
5. **Try Manual Entry:** If scanning fails, you can always enter codes manually

### Calculation Issues

**Problem:** Tax calculation seems incorrect

**Solutions:**
1. **Verify State:** Ensure correct state is selected
2. **Use ZIP Code:** Enter your ZIP code for more accurate rates
3. **Check Tax Rate:** Tax rates are estimates; verify with official sources
4. **Contact Support:** If consistently incorrect, contact support with details

**Problem:** Discount not applying correctly

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Format:** Ensure discount is entered correctly
   - Percentage: Enter number only (e.g., "20" for 20%)
   - Fixed: Enter amount (e.g., "5.00" for $5)
2. **Check Coupon:** Verify coupon code is correct
3. **Check Minimum:** Some coupons require minimum purchase
4. **Try AI Interpretation:** Complex coupons are automatically interpreted by AI

### AI Features Not Working

**Problem:** AI suggestions not loading

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Internet:** Ensure you have internet connection
2. **Check API Key:** If using your own API key, verify it's valid
3. **Wait:** AI responses may take a few seconds
4. **Retry:** Tap the AI button again
5. **Check Rate Limits:** API may have rate limits

**Problem:** AI giving incorrect suggestions

**Solutions:**
1. **AI Limitations:** AI suggestions are for informational purposes
2. **Verify Information:** Always verify AI suggestions independently
3. **Report Issues:** Contact support if AI consistently provides incorrect information

### Data Issues

**Problem:** Shopping history not saving

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Storage:** Ensure device has sufficient storage
2. **Restart App:** Close and reopen the app
3. **Check Permissions:** Ensure app has necessary permissions
4. **Contact Support:** If issue persists, contact support

**Problem:** Lost shopping data

**Solutions:**
1. **Check History:** Look in History view for saved sessions
2. **Auto-Save:** App auto-saves every 30 seconds
3. **Data Recovery:** Contact support for assistance (may not always be possible)

### App Crashes

**Problem:** App crashes or freezes

**Solutions:**
1. **Close and Reopen:** Force close the app and reopen
2. **Restart Device:** Restart your iPhone/iPad
3. **Update App:** Ensure you're using the latest version
4. **Check iOS Version:** Ensure iOS 17.0 or later
5. **Report:** Contact support with crash details

### Performance Issues

**Problem:** App is slow or laggy

**Solutions:**
1. **Close Other Apps:** Close background apps
2. **Restart Device:** Restart your device
3. **Check Storage:** Ensure sufficient storage space
4. **Update iOS:** Ensure latest iOS version
5. **Large Cart:** Very large carts (100+ items) may be slower

### Network Issues

**Problem:** Features requiring internet not working

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Connection:** Ensure Wi-Fi or cellular data is active
2. **Check Settings:** Verify app has network permissions
3. **Try Again:** Network issues may be temporary
4. **Offline Mode:** Core features work offline; AI features require internet

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Best Results

1. **Use ZIP Code:** More accurate tax rates than state averages
2. **Scan Barcodes:** Faster than manual entry
3. **Use AI Suggestions:** Get money-saving tips
4. **Save Sessions:** Your cart auto-saves, but you can manually save important sessions
5. **Export Receipts:** Share receipts for record-keeping

### Privacy Tips

1. **Local Storage:** All data is stored locally on your device
2. **Camera:** Camera is only used for scanning; images aren't stored
3. **AI Data:** Shopping cart data sent to AI is not stored by AI services
4. **Clear Data:** You can clear all data anytime in Settings

### Performance Tips

1. **Keep App Updated:** Always use the latest version
2. **Clear Old History:** Periodically clear old shopping sessions
3. **Close When Not Using:** Close the app when not in use
4. **Manage Storage:** Keep device storage free for best performance
