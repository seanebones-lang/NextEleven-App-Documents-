## Subscription Management

### Purchasing a Subscription

1. Open the Shopper app
2. Navigate to Settings
3. Tap "Upgrade to Premium" or "Subscription"
4. Select your preferred plan (Monthly or Annual)
5. Confirm purchase through Apple's payment system
6. Subscription will activate immediately

### Free Trial

**Planned Feature:** 7-day free trial for new Premium subscribers
- Full access to all Premium features
- No charge during trial period
- Automatically converts to paid subscription after trial
- Cancel anytime during trial to avoid charges

### Managing Your Subscription

**Through the App:**
- Go to Settings → Subscription
- View current subscription status
- Manage renewal settings

**Through Apple:**
- Open Settings app on your iPhone/iPad
- Tap your name → Subscriptions
- Find "Shopper" in your subscriptions list
- Manage, modify, or cancel your subscription

### Subscription Renewal

- Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
- Your Apple ID account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period
- You can turn off auto-renewal at any time in your Apple ID account settings

## Cancellation Policy

### How to Cancel

**Through the App:**
1. Open Shopper
2. Go to Settings → Subscription
3. Tap "Manage Subscription"
4. Follow prompts to cancel

**Through Apple:**
1. Open Settings app
2. Tap your name → Subscriptions
3. Select "Shopper"
4. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

### Cancellation Effects

- **Immediate Cancellation:** You will lose access to Premium features immediately
- **End of Period Cancellation:** You will retain Premium features until the end of your current billing period
- **Data Retention:** Your shopping history and data will remain on your device
- **No Refunds:** Cancellation does not entitle you to a refund for the current billing period
