- **91+ AI Features**: Industry-leading feature set
- **Zero Tracking**: No analytics, no data collection, no third-party tracking
- **On-Device Processing**: AI runs entirely on your device

### What devices are supported?

- iPhone (iOS 17.0 or later)

- Requires camera access for scanning

Yes. Your data is:

## Scanning Receipts

### How do I scan a receipt?

1. Open the app
2. Tap the Scan tab (or camera icon)
3. Point your camera at the receipt
4. Ensure good lighting and hold device steady
5. The app automatically captures when ready
6. Review and save the extracted information

### What makes a good scan?

- **Good Lighting**: Natural light or bright, even lighting
- **Flat Receipt**: Smooth out wrinkles and folds
- **Perpendicular Angle**: Hold device directly above receipt
- **Stable Hands**: Keep device steady during capture
- **Contrast**: Dark text on light background works best

### What types of documents can I scan?

- Receipts (retail, restaurant, online)
- Invoices
- Bills
- Contracts
- Business expenses
- Personal documents
- Any document with text

### How accurate is the scanning?

- **OCR Accuracy**: 99% with Grok Vision
- **Amount Extraction**: 97% accuracy
- **Date Recognition**: 95% accuracy
- **Merchant Detection**: 93% accuracy

AI-powered verification helps catch and correct errors automatically.

### Can I scan multiple receipts at once?

Yes! With a premium subscription, you can:
- Batch scan multiple receipts
- Auto-capture multiple items in sequence
- Bulk import from photo library

2. Tap your **Apple ID** (top)

4. Select **Scanner App by NextEleven**

Or visit: https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

Apple typically responds within 48 hours.

**Solutions:**

- Re-scan with better quality
