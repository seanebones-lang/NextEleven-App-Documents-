## 3. Payment and Billing

### 3.1 Payment Method

- Payment is charged to your **Apple ID account**
- Charges appear on your Apple ID payment method
- Payment is processed by Apple, not directly by us

### 3.2 Billing Cycle

**Monthly Subscription:**
- Charged every month on the same date
- Example: If you subscribe on January 15, you'll be charged on February 15, March 15, etc.

**Yearly Subscription:**
- Charged every year on the same date
- Example: If you subscribe on January 15, you'll be charged on January 15 of the following year

### 3.3 Auto-Renewal

- Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled
- You must cancel at least **24 hours before** the end of the current period
- Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period
- You will receive a notification before renewal

### 3.4 Price Changes

- We reserve the right to change subscription prices
- Existing subscribers will be notified of price changes at least 30 days in advance
- Price changes take effect at the next renewal period
- You can cancel before the price change takes effect

## 4. Subscription Management

### 4.1 How to Manage Your Subscription

**On iOS Device:**
1. Open **Settings**
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find **Zombie Parrot**
5. Manage or cancel your subscription

**On Mac:**
1. Open **App Store**
2. Click your name at the bottom
3. Click **View Information**
4. Scroll to **Subscriptions**
5. Click **Manage**
6. Find **Zombie Parrot** and manage

### 4.2 Cancellation

**How to Cancel:**
- Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Zombie Parrot
- Tap **Cancel Subscription**
- Confirm cancellation

**When Cancellation Takes Effect:**
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
- You retain access to Pro features until the period ends
- No refunds for the current period (you keep access until it ends)

**After Cancellation:**
- Your subscription will not renew
- You'll lose access to Pro features at the end of the current period
- You can resubscribe at any time

### 4.3 Restore Purchases

If you've previously subscribed:
1. Open Zombie Parrot
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Restore Purchases**
4. Your subscription will be restored if still active
