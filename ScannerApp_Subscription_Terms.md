# Scanner App by NextEleven - Subscription Terms

**Last Updated: December 2025**

## Overview

**Scanner App by NextEleven** offers auto-renewable subscriptions that provide access to premium features. This document details all subscription terms, pricing, billing, and cancellation policies.

## Subscription Plans

### Free Tier (No Subscription Required)

**Features:**
- 10 scans per month (receipts and documents combined)
- Basic AI categorization using on-device processing
- Local storage only (no iCloud sync)
- Limited analytics dashboard
- Basic receipt organization
- Manual data entry and editing

**Limitations:**
- No advanced AI features
- No batch processing
- No PDF export
- No unlimited scans
- No priority support

### Monthly Premium Subscription

**Price:** $4.99 per month

**Product ID:** `com.mothershipai.scannerapp.monthly`

**Features:**
- Unlimited receipt and document scanning
- Advanced AI categorization and analysis
- Full analytics dashboard with insights
- PDF report generation
- Batch processing for multiple receipts
- iCloud sync across all your devices
- Return deadline reminders
- Advanced search and filtering
- Email support

**Billing:**
- Charged monthly to your Apple ID account
- Automatically renews unless cancelled 24 hours before renewal
- First charge occurs at purchase confirmation
- Subsequent charges occur 24 hours before each renewal date

### Pro Vault (Yearly Subscription)

**Price:** $49.99 per year

**Product ID:** `com.mothershipai.scannerappannual`

**Features:**
- All Monthly Premium features
- Priority email support
- Early access to new features
- Advanced export options
- Extended data retention
- Best value (saves 17% compared to monthly)

**Billing:**
- Charged annually to your Apple ID account
- Automatically renews unless cancelled 24 hours before renewal
- First charge occurs at purchase confirmation
- Subsequent charges occur 24 hours before each annual renewal date

## Free Trial

### Trial Details

- **Duration**: 7 days
- **Eligibility**: New subscribers only (first-time subscribers)
- **Features**: Full access to all premium features during trial
- **Start Date**: Begins immediately upon subscription

### Trial Billing

- **No Charge**: Free for the first 7 days
- **Auto-Conversion**: After 7 days, automatically converts to paid subscription
- **Cancellation**: Cancel anytime during trial to avoid charges
- **Notification**: You'll receive reminder before trial ends

### Important Trial Notes

1. To avoid charges, cancel at least 24 hours before trial ends
2. Cancelling during trial gives you access until trial end date
3. Trial is available once per Apple ID
4. Trial offer may change or end without notice

## Auto-Renewal Terms

### How Auto-Renewal Works

1. **Initial Purchase**: You purchase a subscription (or start free trial)
2. **Automatic Renewal**: Subscription renews automatically at end of billing period
3. **Charge Timing**: Account charged 24 hours before renewal date
4. **Cancellation Window**: Must cancel 24 hours before renewal to avoid charge
5. **Continued Access**: Access continues until end of paid period, even after cancellation

### Renewal Notifications

You will receive notifications:
- 7 days before renewal
- 24 hours before renewal
- Immediately after renewal charge

### Managing Auto-Renewal

Turn off auto-renewal at any time:
1. Open **Settings** on your iOS device
2. Tap your **Apple ID** (top of Settings)
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Select **Scanner App by NextEleven**
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**

Or manage at: https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

## Cancellation Policy

### How to Cancel

**Method 1: iOS Device**
1. Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
2. Select Scanner App by NextEleven
3. Tap Cancel Subscription

**Method 2: Mac**
1. App Store → Account → View Information
2. Manage → Subscriptions
3. Select Scanner App by NextEleven → Cancel

**Method 3: Web**
1. Visit https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Find Scanner App by NextEleven
4. Click Cancel Subscription

### Cancellation Terms

- **Effective Date**: End of current billing period
- **Immediate Effect**: No immediate loss of access
- **Access Duration**: Access continues until period ends
- **No Partial Refunds**: No refunds for unused portion of subscription
- **Reactivation**: Can reactivate anytime before period ends

### After Cancellation

When your subscription ends:
- You revert to Free Tier (10 scans per month)
- All previously scanned data remains accessible
- Premium features become locked
- iCloud sync stops (local data remains)
- Can export data before subscription ends

### When Refunds May Be Denied

Refunds typically not granted for:
