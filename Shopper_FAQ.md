## General Questions

### What is Shopper?

Shopper is a mobile shopping calculator app that helps you:
- Calculate shopping totals with discounts and coupons
- Apply accurate state-based sales tax
- Scan barcodes for quick product entry
- Scan QR codes and barcodes for coupon codes
- Get AI-powered shopping suggestions
- Save shopping history
- Generate PDF receipts

### Is Shopper free?

Shopper offers a free tier with basic features. Premium features are available through subscription plans (coming soon).

### What devices are supported?

- iPhone running iOS 17.0 or later
- iPad running iPadOS 17.0 or later

### Do I need an account?

No, Shopper does not require an account. All data is stored locally on your device.

### Is my data private?

Yes! All shopping data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

## Features & Usage

### How do I add items to my cart?

1. Enter the item price in the "Add Item" section
2. Optionally add a discount (percentage or fixed amount)
3. Tap "Add Item"

### Can I scan barcodes?

Yes! Tap the barcode icon next to the price field to scan product barcodes. The app will automatically look up the product and enter the price.

### How do I scan coupons?

Tap the QR code icon in the "Coupon Code" section, then point your camera at the coupon QR code or barcode.

### How does tax calculation work?

You can calculate tax in two ways:
1. **By State:** Select your state from the dropdown for average state tax rates
2. **By ZIP Code:** Enter your ZIP code for more accurate local tax rates

**Note:** Tax rates are estimates. Always verify with official sources.

### What types of coupons are supported?

Shopper supports:
- Percentage discounts (e.g., "20% OFF")
- Fixed amount discounts (e.g., "$10 OFF")
- Conditional discounts (e.g., "$10 OFF $50")
- BOGO (Buy One Get One) offers
- Free shipping codes

### How does AI work?

Shopper uses xAI's Grok API to provide:
- Shopping suggestions and money-saving tips
- Automatic coupon code interpretation
- Cart optimization recommendations

### Can I save my shopping sessions?

Yes! Your cart automatically saves every 30 seconds. You can also view and resume past sessions from the History view.

### Can I export receipts?

Yes! Tap the "Share" button to generate and share a PDF receipt via Messages, Email, AirDrop, or other sharing options.

## Technical Questions

### Why does the app need camera access?

Camera access is used to:
- Scan product barcodes for quick entry
- Scan QR codes and barcodes for coupon codes

**Important:** Images are processed on-device and are not stored or transmitted.

### Do I need internet connection?

- **Core Features:** Work offline (calculations, cart management)
- **AI Features:** Require internet (suggestions, coupon interpretation)
- **Barcode Lookup:** Requires internet (product information)
- **Tax Lookup:** Requires internet (ZIP code-based rates)

### What data is sent to AI services?

Only shopping cart data (items, prices, discounts) is sent to xAI's Grok API for processing. No personal information is included.

### Is my shopping data synced to the cloud?

Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. Cloud sync may be available in future updates.

### Can I use my own xAI API key?

Yes! You can provide your own xAI API key for AI features. The app also includes an internal API key for convenience.

## Troubleshooting

### The camera preview is black. What should I do?

1. Check camera permissions in iOS Settings → Shopper → Camera
2. Restart the app
3. Restart your device
4. Ensure you're running iOS 17.0 or later

### Barcodes aren't scanning. What's wrong?

- Ensure adequate lighting
- Hold device 6-12 inches from barcode
- Wait for camera to focus
- Clean your camera lens
- Try manual entry if scanning fails

### Tax calculation seems wrong. Why?

- Tax rates are estimates based on available data
- Use ZIP code for more accurate rates
- Verify with official tax sources
- Contact support if consistently incorrect

### AI features aren't working. What should I do?

1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify API key is valid (if using your own)
3. Wait a few seconds for response
4. Try again
5. Check for API rate limits

### The app crashes. How do I fix it?

1. Close and reopen the app
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest app version
4. Ensure iOS 17.0 or later
5. Contact support with crash details

### My shopping history disappeared. Can I recover it?

- Check the History view for saved sessions
- Data is stored locally, so recovery depends on device storage
- Contact support for assistance (recovery may not always be possible)
