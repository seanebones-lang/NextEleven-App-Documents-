# Ultimate Life Coach - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Last Updated:** December 24, 2025

## General Questions

### What is Ultimate Life Coach?

Ultimate Life Coach is an AI-powered life coaching app that provides personalized emotional support and guidance. It uses Grok 4.1 Fast Reasoning to have meaningful conversations, track your mood, and help you grow personally.

### How does it work?

The app uses AI to have conversations with you, remember your history, and provide personalized coaching. You can chat via text or voice, track your mood, and save meaningful insights.

### Is it free?

The app offers a 7-day free trial. After the trial, a subscription is required to continue using the app.

### What devices does it work on?

- **Platform:** iOS 17.0 or later
- **Devices:** iPhone and iPad
- **Internet:** Required for AI features (optional for viewing history)

## Subscription Questions

### How much does it cost?

- **Monthly:** Price displayed in App Store (varies by region)
- **Annual:** Price displayed in App Store (savings vs. monthly)
- **Free Trial:** 7 days free

### How does the free trial work?

- **Duration:** 7 days
- **Access:** Full premium features during trial
- **Charge:** No charge during trial
- **Cancellation:** Cancel anytime during trial to avoid charges
- **Auto-Renewal:** Converts to paid subscription unless cancelled

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Open Settings app on your device
2. Tap your Apple ID (top of screen)
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Find "Ultimate Life Coach"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

### Can I get a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple according to their refund policy. Contact Apple Support for refund requests.

### Can I share my subscription?

Subscriptions may be shareable through Apple's Family Sharing program, subject to Apple's terms.

## Features Questions

### What features are included?

All subscriptions include:
- Unlimited AI conversations
- All role-play modes (normal, future self, historical figures)
- Mood tracking and insights
- Voice input and output
- Photo analysis
- Insight journal
- Conversation history
- Built-in API key

### What are role-play modes?

You can chat with:
- **Normal Mode:** Standard life coach
- **Future Self:** Chat with your future self
- **Historical Figures:** Chat with historical figures like Marcus Aurelius, Rumi, etc.

### How does mood tracking work?

- Log your mood daily (or anytime)
- Add optional notes
- View trends over time
- Get insights based on your mood patterns

### Can I use my own API key?

Yes! Go to Settings → API Configuration to enter your own xAI API key. This is optional - the app includes a default key.

### How does voice work?

- **Input:** Tap microphone to speak (transcribed to text)
- **Output:** AI responses can be spoken aloud
- **Toggle:** Enable/disable in Settings

### Can I analyze photos?

Yes! You can share photos for emotional analysis. Photos are sent to xAI API for processing (not stored by us).

## Technical Questions

### Why isn't the app working?

Common issues:
- Check internet connection
- Restart the app
- Update to latest version
- Check subscription status
- Contact support: info@mothership-ai.com

### The app keeps crashing

- Update to latest version
- Restart your device
- Check available storage
- Reinstall the app (your data is stored locally)
- Contact support with details

### API errors or rate limiting

- **Rate Limited:** Wait a few minutes and try again
- **Invalid Key:** Check your API key in Settings
- **Default Key Down:** Try using your own API key
- **Network Issues:** Check your internet connection

### How do I backup my data?

- **Export:** Settings → Export Chat History
- **Local:** Data is stored on your device
- **iCloud:** Not synced by default (iOS may backup if enabled)

### Can I use it offline?

- **View History:** Yes, you can view past conversations offline
- **New Messages:** No, internet required for AI features
- **Mood Tracking:** Yes, works offline (syncs when online)

## Privacy Questions

### Is my data private?

Yes! All data is stored locally on your device. We do not access, view, or store your conversations, moods, or insights.

### Where is my data stored?

- **Location:** On your device only
- **Format:** Local JSON files
- **Backup:** Not backed up by us (iOS may backup if enabled)
- **Sync:** Not synced to our servers

### Do you track me?

No. We do not:
- Track your usage
- Collect analytics
- Use third-party trackers
- Share data with advertisers

### What about the API?

- Messages are sent to xAI API for processing
- Photos are sent to xAI API for analysis
- Subject to xAI's privacy policy
- We do not store API data

### Can I delete my data?

Yes! Settings → Delete All Data. **Warning:** This is permanent and cannot be undone.

## Troubleshooting

### App won't open

- Force close and reopen
- Restart device

- Reinstall app
